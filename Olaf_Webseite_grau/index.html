About this game

Join Olaf on his journey through a strange world. A world in which you can only move if you manipulate gravity. So forget the traditional way of moving — because you can't do that anyway.
This 3D puzzle tests not only your skill, but also your logical thinking. A dangerous journey lies ahead of you and don't even bother, there is no one to help you. Solve puzzles or find keys and try to make it out alive! Because a penguin is out to get you and he knows no half measures — except maybe cutting you in half.
